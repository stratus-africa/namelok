

## Namelok Wireless — New Website Plan

### Design Direction
- **Warm & community-focused** palette: earthy tones inspired by the Kenyan landscape
  - Primary: terracotta/warm brown (`#B85042`)
  - Secondary: sand/cream (`#E7E8D1`)
  - Accent: sage green (`#A7BEAE`)
  - Dark sections: deep brown (`#3D2B1F`)
- Rounded, friendly UI elements with generous whitespace
- Community photography as hero background (from existing site assets)

### Single-Page Structure (scroll sections with sticky nav)

1. **Navigation Bar** — Logo, section links (About, Services, Coverage, Impact, Partners, Contact), "Client Portal" button linking to billing.namelok.net

2. **Hero Section** — Full-width background image, headline "Enda Sopa Pooki", subtitle "Powering Connectivity, Empowering Communities", CTA buttons: "View Coverage" + "Get Connected"

3. **About / Who We Are** — Brief intro text about being a community-rooted CISP in Kajiado West. Key stats in large callout cards: 250+ Homes, 4 Schools, 4 Health Centres, 7 Communities

4. **The Challenge** — 4-card grid showing the digital divide: No Affordable Access, Schools Left Behind, Healthcare Disconnected, Economic Exclusion

5. **Services** — Icon + text cards in a 2×3 grid: Community Hotspots, Dedicated Access Points, School Connectivity, Healthcare Connectivity, Digital Literacy, Smart Solutions (CCTV, irrigation, LMS)

6. **How It Works** — 4-step visual flow: Backhaul → Distribution → Community Access → Revenue Reinvested

7. **Coverage Map** — List of 7 currently connected communities + 5 expansion areas (Coming Soon badges), embedded Google Maps coverage link

8. **Impact & Social Enterprise** — Stats display + Digital Literacy Programme details + Smart Community Solutions

9. **Vision** — Quote block with the vision statement, 3 pillars: Digital Equity, Service Equality, Local Ownership

10. **Partners** — Logo row for KICTANET, Help NGO, Internet Society, with "Why Partner" reasons

11. **Contact Section** — Working contact form (name, email, phone, message, dropdown for Get Connected/Get Support/General) powered by Lovable Cloud email. Also displays email (info@namelokwireless.co.ke) and location info.

12. **Footer** — Logo, quick links, social links, copyright

### Contact Form
- Will set up Lovable Cloud with email infrastructure to send confirmation emails to users and notification emails to Namelok's team

### Mobile Responsive
- Hamburger menu on mobile
- Stacked cards and sections
- Touch-friendly spacing
